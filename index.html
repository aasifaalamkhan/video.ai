Video.ai - Data Deletion Instructions

App Name: Video.ai

Developer Contact: mai.imotion.ai@gmail.com

Per Google Play policies, you have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. Below are the methods available to you.

Method 1: Immediate In-App Deletion (Recommended)

This is the fastest way to delete your data. It is automated and instant.

  1. Open the Video.ai app on your device.
  2. Login to your account.
  3. Go to the Profile tab.
  4. Tap the Menu icon (three dots or vertical lines) in the top-right corner.
  5. Select Delete Account.
  6. Confirm the warning prompt.

Method 2: Request via Email

If you cannot access the app or have uninstalled it, you may request deletion via email.

  1. Send an email to: mai.imotion.ai@gmail.com
  2. Subject Line: Account Deletion Request
  3. Body: Please include the email address associated with your account or your user ID if known.

Note: Manual email requests may take up to 7 business days to process.

What Data is Deleted?

Warning: Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Account Info: Your User ID, email address, and profile photo.
  • Generated Content: All video tasks, prompt history, and generated video files stored on our servers.
  • Credits: Any remaining Token balance or Subscription status.

Data Retention Policy

We do not retain deleted data. Once the deletion process is triggered (either in-app or via admin), all personal identifiers and content are purged from our databases (Firestore) and storage buckets (Backblaze B2) immediately or within a short automated cleanup cycle.
