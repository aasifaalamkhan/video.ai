Privacy Policy for BabyDream: AI Baby Generator

This Privacy Policy explains how BabyDream collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the BabyDream mobile app and related services.

Last updated: July 18, 2026 App name: BabyDream Play Store title: BabyDream: AI Baby Generator

1. Who We Are

BabyDream is an AI-powered app that lets users create baby prediction images, future family portraits, family similarity visuals, photo edits, and related creative content.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

  • Photos you upload for AI generation or editing.
  • Account details if you connect with Google.
  • Support messages, feedback, or reports you submit.
  • Purchase and credit-related activity inside the app.

Information collected automatically

  • Device and app usage data.
  • App diagnostics, crash data, and performance logs.
  • Anonymous identifiers and internal user IDs.

3. How We Use Information

  • To create and deliver AI-generated images requested by you.
  • To save your tasks, credits, purchases, and generation history.
  • To let you use guest mode and later back up your account with Google.
  • To improve app performance, reliability, and abuse prevention.
  • To respond to support requests, safety complaints, or reports.

4. Services and Third Parties We Use

BabyDream may use the following service providers to operate the app:

  • Firebase Authentication for sign-in and account management.
  • Cloud Firestore to store user profiles, tasks, credits, and metadata.
  • Firebase Functions to process secure backend requests.
  • Backblaze B2 to store uploaded and generated media files.
  • BytePlus Seedream to generate or edit AI images.
  • Google Sign-In if you choose to connect your Google account.

These providers may process data on our behalf only to the extent needed to deliver the features of the app.

5. Photos and AI Processing

When you upload photos, those images may be transmitted to our backend infrastructure and AI processing providers to generate results you request.

  • Uploaded photos may be temporarily or persistently stored.
  • Generated outputs may be saved to your task history and profile.
  • Source thumbnails may appear in task cards and previews for usability.

6. Guest Mode and Account Backup

You may use BabyDream in guest mode. If you later connect with Google, your guest activity may be linked or migrated to your saved account so your generations, credits, and purchases remain available.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share data only in limited situations such as:

  • With service providers needed to operate the app.
  • To comply with legal obligations or valid government requests.
  • To investigate abuse, fraud, security, or policy violations.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or transfer of business assets.

8. Data Retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service, maintain your account, complete requested tasks, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.

Retention periods may vary depending on the type of data, storage system, and whether you delete your account.

9. Your Choices and Rights

  • You can choose whether to connect with Google.
  • You can request deletion of your profile from within the app if that feature is enabled.
  • You can delete content, where available, from your saved task history.
  • You can stop using the app at any time.

Depending on your location, you may also have rights to access, correct, delete, or object to certain processing of your personal information.

10. Children's Privacy

BabyDream is not intended for children to use on their own without permission from a parent or legal guardian. If you believe personal data has been provided inappropriately by a child, contact us so we can review and delete it where appropriate.

11. Security
